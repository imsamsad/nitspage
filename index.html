Welcome to NIT Silchar

National Institute of Technology (NIT) Silchar is one of the 31 National Institutes of Technology in India and was established in 1967. It is located in Silchar, Assam. The institute is known for its strong emphasis on technical education and research.The campus is equipped with modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, classrooms, hostels, sports facilities, and a library. The institute focuses on providing a conducive environment for academic and extracurricular activities.
